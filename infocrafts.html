OVERVIEW



BACKGROUND

I have been a fan of Blizzard Entertainment's StarCraft franchise for more than half my life. To improve my skills, I often watch YouTube videos created by my favorite video game personality, Sean "Day[9]" Plott (the cover photo is a picture I took of him, the left caster, at MLG Anaheim!). I, and many others who have seen Sean's videos, all recognize the quality of insightful content he provides when teaching people how to become better gamers. However, his videos are somewhat long to account for all of the information.

CONCEPT

I wanted these infographics to be memory joggers for Day9's videos. But I also wanted to enhance the content by through encoding, visual hierarchy, color, and shape associations. This helps absorb the information more efficiently.
