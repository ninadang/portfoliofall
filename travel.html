How Far Have We Travelled?

Info Visualization Project




VISUAL DESIGN

OVERVIEW



BACKGROUND

Space travel has always been a fascinating field to me. In my Information Visualization and Aesthetics course, I chose this as the topic for an infographic I would spend the quarter working on.

CONCEPT

This infographic explores the history of space travel under the topic of distance. The colors and design elements were chosen to create a dark, futuristic-inspired aesthetic.

Project Details

Nina Dang
Instructor: Marilyn Ostergren
10 Weeks

RESEARCH

RESEARCH INTO THE SPACE

I found color, text, and layout models that I emulated with my infographic. My data sources mainly relied on data from NASA's spacecraft sites, as well as information about earth-orbiting satellites.

DESIGN

STRATEGY

Five forms of visual potential were utilized in my infographic: appearance, numbers, location, time, and relationships. Every week we were introduced to one of these concepts and created two displays that incorporated them. The displays underwent two rounds of critique and were then selected to compile the final infographic.
